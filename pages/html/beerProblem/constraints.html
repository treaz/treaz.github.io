Constraints of the model

The constraints for this model:

The amount of beer transported from each brewery should be at most the supply of the brewery.
sum(X_Haarlem_c) <= supply_Haarlem, c={Amsterdam, Amersfoort, Gouda}
sum(X_Eindhoven_c) <= supply_Eindhoven, c={Amsterdam, Amersfoort, Gouda}

The amount of beer transported to each customer should be at least than the demand of the customer.
sum(X_b_Amsterdam) >= demand_Amsterdam, c={Haarlem, Eindhoven}
sum(X_b_Amersfoort) >= demand_Amersfoort, c={Haarlem, Eindhoven}
sum(X_b_Gouda) >= demand_Gouda, c={Haarlem, Eindhoven}
