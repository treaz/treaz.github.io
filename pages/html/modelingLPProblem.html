Reaching a methodology for modeling any linear programming (LP) problem

Let's take a look at 2 different linear programming problems:

Bus driver allocation
Chair transport

A pattern emerges for creating an optimization model for a LP problem:
Identify decision variables
Identify the objective function
Identify the constraints
Identify the non-negativity constraints
Two auxiliary concepts to this methodology that have introduced during the examples are: parameters and optimal solution.
