Non-negativity constraints

Description

A special kind of constraint. In most practical problems most variables are required to be nonnegative.

Quiz

For the chips problem presented before, can you identify the non-negativity constraints?

The units of plain chips produced (X_plain) cannot be negative.
The units of mexican chips produced (X_mexican) cannot be negative.
X_i >= 0; i = {plain, mexican}
