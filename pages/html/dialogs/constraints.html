For the chips problem (opens in new page) presented before, can you identify the constraints?

The time required to slice the plain chips (TS_plain) plus the time required to slice the Mexican chips (TS_mexican) must be less than or equal to the time the slicer is available (TSa), sum (X_i*TS_i) <= TSa; i = {plain, mexican}

The time required to fry the plain chips (TF_plain) plus the time required to fry the Mexican chips (TF_mexican) must be less than or equal to the time the fryer is available (TFa), sum(X_i*TF_i) <= TFa; i = {plain, mexican}

The time required to package the plain chips (TP_plain) plus the time required to package the Mexican chips (TP_mexican) must be less than or equal to the time the packager is available (TPa), sum(X_i*TP_i) <= TPa; i = {plain, mexican}
