Mathematical model

Description

An abstract model that describes, in general mathematical terms, the relations contained in a model. A model is defined as an abstract representation of reality. In the current context, a representation of a decision problem related to the operations of the organization.

Resources

The creation of a mathematical model is usually not a simple task, requiring knowledge and experience (usually acquired through academic courses). The following resources provide a starting point.
A tutorial on mathematical modeling
