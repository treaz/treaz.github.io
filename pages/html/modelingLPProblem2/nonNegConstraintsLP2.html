Non-negativity constraints

Description

A special kind of constraint. In most practical problems most variables are required to be nonnegative.

Example (from the bus scheduling problem)

  • The non-negativity constraints ensure the number if allocated drivers for an interval is not negative.
  • Mathematical notation: Xi>=0, i={0, 4, 8,.. 20}
