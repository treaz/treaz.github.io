Decision variables

Description

The quantities that the decision makers would like to determine. They are the unknowns of a mathematical programming model.

Example (from the bus scheduling problem)

  • The decision variable is the number of drivers allocated every 4 hours: X0, X4, X8, X12, X16, X20.
  • Mathematical notation: Xi, i={0, 4, 8,.. 20}
